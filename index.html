Raghunath Learning Centre (RLC)
The premier institute for preparing for GATE exam in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE). Located in Bangalore, RLC provides concept-oriented GATE coaching. The focus is on both the depth of understanding of the concepts, and innovative problem solving techniques. The subjects are taught by Dr. K Raghunath, who has a PhD from IISc Bangalore.
